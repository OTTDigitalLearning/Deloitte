About us

What the modern learner demands from us:

  • More micro learnings:   A limited attention span requires attention well used. Learning nuggets should not exceed six minutes per nugget. 
  • An aligned and productive workplace:   Disruptions need to be avoided or limited. 
  • More mobile learning:   Step away from classroom and LMS based learning offers only. The modern learner is ‘always on’ and requires content whenever and wherever needed. 
  • More learning opportunities:   Time to spend on training is not sufficient. Why? Because learning nowadays still requires stepping back from business obligations. We need to embed learning into business, into the flow of work. 
  • Acknowledgement of the willingness to learn:   Offer a greater variety of different learning possibilities, e.g. micro and macro, formal and informal learning etc.
